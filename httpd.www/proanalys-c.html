Instructions

Welcome to participate in a comprehensive personality analysis!

  • The purpose of this questionnaire is to provide insight into your core personality.
  • Answer the questions based on how you typically think and behave.
  • Your answers should reflect who you are as a person, not how you act in your current or future job role.
  • There are no right or wrong answers. Every choice is valid.
  • All personality types are valuable, each with its own strengths and growth opportunities. This analysis aims to identify yours.
  • Try not to consider how you were raised or trained to behave in order to fit in.
  • Disregard others' expectations and answer in a way that truly reflects who you are when you’re being yourself.
  • We share similarities with some people and differences with others. This analysis is designed to capture that uniqueness.
